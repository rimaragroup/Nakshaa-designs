The studio

I don't just design rooms — I design lifestyles. Every space carries a story; my work is to draw it out and build it beautifully.

Led by Kashmira Jain, Nakshaa is a design practice working across India and internationally — from first sketch to handed-over home. We treat the plan, the light, the materials and the way a day moves through a room as one connected idea.

01

Functional

Layouts that work for how you actually live — flow, light and storage resolved before anything is beautiful.

02

Beautiful

A considered balance of aesthetics and detail. Swatches that become stories, finishes that feel inevitable.

03

Timeless

Interiors designed to outlast trends — calm, confident spaces you grow into rather than out of.
